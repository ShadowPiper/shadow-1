What is it?

Introduction

The Great Highland Bagpipe is a large instrument known for its unique sound and music. The music of the Great Highland Bagpipe (or GHB) comes in a number of different styles. Here we will look at some of the fundamental concepts to understand how bagpipes work, to see the different types of music, and to see how this instrument of tunes.

From an outside perspective, the bagpipes may seem more complex than they really are. Some people seem to think the melody comes out of the drones, though this is not possible.
